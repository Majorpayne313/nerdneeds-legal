Community Guidelines & Safety Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Nerd Needs is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive community for all users. These guidelines outline our expectations for user behavior and our policies against harmful content.

‚ö†Ô∏è Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Nerd Needs has ZERO TOLERANCE for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any form of child exploitation.

We strictly prohibit:

  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming behavior or solicitation of minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAM
  • Any attempt to meet minors for sexual purposes
  • Content that sexualizes minors in any way

All users must be 18 years or older to use Nerd Needs.

We use automated systems and human review to detect and remove violating content. All instances of CSAE are reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.

Violations result in immediate permanent ban and report to law enforcement.

üö´ Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Nerd Needs:

  • Harassment & Bullying: Any behavior intended to intimidate, demean, or harm another user
  • Hate Speech: Content targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any protected characteristic
  • Threats & Violence: Threats of violence or harm to oneself or others
  • Non-consensual Intimate Images: Sharing intimate images without consent
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be someone else or catfishing
  • Scams & Fraud: Spam, financial fraud, or solicitation of money
  • Illegal Activities: Promoting illegal activities or controlled substances
  • Sexual Harassment: Unwanted sexual advances or explicit content
  • Stalking: Repeated unwanted contact or monitoring of another user

üí¨ Verbal Abuse & Bullying Policy

We do not tolerate verbal abuse in any form, including:

  • Insults, name-calling, or degrading language
  • Body shaming or appearance-based harassment
  • Mocking someone's interests, hobbies, or passions
  • Repeated negative comments intended to upset someone
  • Sharing private conversations to embarrass someone
  • Encouraging others to harass a user
  • Discriminatory remarks or slurs

‚úÖ Expected Behavior

We expect all Nerd Needs users to:

  • Treat others with respect and dignity
  • Accept "no" as an answer gracefully
  • Keep conversations appropriate and consensual
  • Respect boundaries and personal space
  • Be honest in your profile and conversations
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior

üö© How to Report Violations

If you encounter any violation of these guidelines:

  1. Tap the "..." menu on the user's profile or in chat
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose the reason for your report
  4. Provide any additional details
  5. Submit your report

Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety concerns, please also contact local authorities.

‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement & Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Violating content will be removed
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated or serious violations
  • Permanent Ban: Severe violations or repeated offenses
  • Law Enforcement Report: Illegal activity, including CSAE

The severity of action depends on the nature and frequency of violations. CSAE violations result in immediate permanent ban and law enforcement notification.
